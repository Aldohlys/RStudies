01Scoring Methodology — 2-Gate System

Gate 1: Technical Analysis

Gate: sector ETF gate passed + stock score ≥ 5
Score (0–10): 10 criteria across 4 themes. TRADE ≥ 7 | WATCH 5–6 | SKIP < 5
Relative Strength 1 pt
  • Stock outperforming (long) or underperforming (short) its sector ETF over 20 days
Trend Structure 4 pts
  • Price above (long) or below (short) MA50
  • MA50 slope rising (long) or declining (short)
  • ADX > 20 with directional index confirming (DI+ > DI- for long, DI- > DI+ for short)
  • Price in upper (long ≥ 55%) or lower (short ≤ 45%) half of 20-day range
Momentum & Asymmetry 3 pts
  • RSI14 above (long) or below (short) 50, with slope confirming direction
  • Not overextended: within 15% of MA50 (room to mean-revert against you)
  • Room to run: price > 1 ATR away from 20-day support (short) or resistance (long)
Volume Confirmation 2 pts
  • On-Balance Volume (OBV) trend aligned with direction over 20 days
  • Up/Down volume ratio: up-volume > down-volume for long (ratio > 1.1), inverse for short (< 0.9)

Gate 2: Optionality

Gate: IV data exists + Optionality ≥ PARTIAL
PASS (3–4) | PARTIAL (2) | FAIL (0–1)
  • IV30 < 40%: favorable level (+1)
  • IVP < 60%: room for IV expansion (+1)
  • VRP < 0: negative vol risk premium (+1)
  • Contango: iv30 < iv180 (+1)
TRADE = both gates pass + top 3 composite (tech + persistence)  |  WATCH = tech gate passed, optionality gate failed (max 10 shown)  |  SKIP = tech gate failed (hidden, in CSV)  |  VIX > 25 = warning banner in macro report (informational only)
